Filtering & Search ​

LazyFlare solves large accounts with instantaneous, in-memory filtering.

How to Filter ​

From anywhere in the app:

  1. Press / to focus the Filter Bar (located right above the data table).
  2. Begin typing.

As you type, the massive data table below will instantly re-render to only show rows that contain your search query. The search is case-insensitive and scans across all columns simultaneously.

Clearing the Filter ​

To exit the filter bar and reset the data table back to its original state, simply press Esc.
